Positive Vibes Of Digital Learning

By Tanisha Bajaj (VIIID)

In these uncertain times,
Filled with struggle and famine;
The generation tried to cope,
With happiness, education, and scope.
Sometimes the day started early,
Sometimes it started late;
Everything became very peculiar,
But our life was changing, mate!
We started using technology,
Combining laptops, phones, and books;
We thought this would never work probably,
But it continued in perfect harmony.
Everything was unexpected,
For the first time we could go nowhere;
The family sat together and recollected
The memories and thoughts we would share.
We discovered new abilities,
We came close to each other;
For the first time, we felt like a perfect family
We mingled with one another.
Mother's day was different this year,
As we all stayed at home in fear;
We all suddenly became grateful to our,
Doctors and nurses, our valiant warriors.
