A NEW ME...

By Parvathi Binu (VIIIC)

One of my favourite hobbies (or maybe my only one) is reading. For me it's like a movie in my mind; using the details and description from the books I create my own imaginary world. Once I start reading I never stop... so now I am going to share with you one of the most popular books. The Famous Five written by Enid Blyton, George, Dick, Anne, Julian and of course faithful Timmy- the book's main character. This brave pack of children go on daring adventures, saving people, getting lost, catching crooks and more. This is not a book review, it is just something that turned my boring "HOLIDAY" at home into a better one. Each of these characters has their own personality: George or Georgina, a fearless explorer, the bravest (and the shortest- tempered) girl in the team. Julian, responsible for his age and a good leader too. Dick, the funniest person even in the hardest times, always keeps the team's mood positive. Anne, the youngest of them all, called the kind protector. Faithful Timmy, the constant companion of George has rescued them from dangerous crooks. Reading made my life interesting during the most boring days, maybe it will make yours too.
