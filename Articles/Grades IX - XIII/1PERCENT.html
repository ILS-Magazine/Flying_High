What The 1% Do That The 99% Aren'T

By Drishti Singh (IXA)

In the world of increasing competition, the urge to be better and leaving an impact is what we thrive for, but other than being successful and making a fortune the 1% of the successful population develop certain habits which set them apart from the rat race.

  • Constantly reading books: Now most of the people spend their free time scrolling through social media, but if you take the example of prosperous personalities like Bill Gates, they spend their free time reading good books.
  • Surrounding yourself with positive people: Many people say that your company has a great effect on you, and successful people know that therefore, they surround themselves with practical and optimistic people.
  • Setting small but daily goals: Setting goals is something which most don't consider worth doing, but it is a great way of reminding ourselves of small achievements and boosting our self-confidence.
  • Pursuing your passion: "Make your passion your work and you don't have to work a single day."" -Jeff Bezoz. Don't force yourself to like something, you will never succeed, choose something you really like, because you may be one of the few pursuing it and you will be successful.
  • Watch your spending: Over two-thirds of the successful people are frugal with their money. They spent their money thoughtfully, not emotionally. 99% of the population spends money recklessly without saving, so my advice for you is to watch how you spend and save for the future.
  • Don't give up: Making mistakes is a part of life, but it should not be the reason you give up on your goal, when you drop down get back up and keep going!
