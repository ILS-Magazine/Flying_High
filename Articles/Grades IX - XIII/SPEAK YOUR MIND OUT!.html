Speak Your Mind Out!

By Ramyashree Rajendra (IXB)

In this world of silenced and lonely days,
The mind is fluttering away like incessant waves.
There is a fascinating desire in the heart,
Mind is filled with lots of questions from lame to smart.
But there seemed to be no start for this heavenly vision,
From that day onwards I was on a mission.
To expose my thoughts in the open air,
Having a thought that there ought to be people who care.


Why is the ocean on the earth so blue?
Why do the leaves look so beautiful adorned with dew?
How does the root keep the soil bound?
How is Mother Nature so helpful all around?
Some of my questions were as simple as these,
I am still on my journey to answer them with ease.
Once thoughts arise, they soon sublime,
New questions emerge with excitement at a new time.


This was how I felt with my mind out,
You can try it too; a fun way about.
Do you wish to open the doors of creativity in you?
If yes, then be curious in whatever you do.
Don't be afraid don't stand still,
Be confident and act wisely to your will.
I feel I must listen to my own advice,
Or perhaps tomorrow I may speak out without thinking twice.
