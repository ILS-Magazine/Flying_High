Speak Your Mind Out

By Ravneet Kaur (IXC)

Our mind is like a curious child who wants to learn more and more about this world. Not every child in this world has the ability to be a bibliophile. According to me, there are two types of children in this world bibliophile and creative artists. Bibliophiles are the ones who know how to draw but are good only at drawing diagrams not their imagination. Creative artists are those who pen down their imaginations as well as maintain a balance between their studies and their imaginative world. Just be creative artists because this world is standing on imagination and creativity. Creativity helps you to look at the world in a different way. My mind has always told me to be creative and imaginative as it helps you to find a solution to any kind of situation. Creative people know this world in a better manner. Whenever they see anything near them then they think how it is made or how it can be made. Assume that you are very good at designing clothes or you are a fashion designer, whenever you see any kind of dress or costume you may think that what could be the measurements, clothes and the procedure used to make the dress. Fashion designers are always busy in thinking or designing an imaginative dress and then they draw and stitch. This is what called an active mind, which is always thinking about something around the person or is busy in creating imaginative stories. Such creative artists are also very good at creating stories, just give any topic to a creative and imaginative person and tell him/her to create a plot or a story, that person will give your something which was out of your thinking. Just be creative and imaginative which also will help you in studies.
