Mathematics

By Rutul Mistry (VB)

Try, try and try,

The more I try,

The more I cry,

I practice math with my heart and soul,

Yet I am not able to achieve my goal.

I never get marks in maths

In unite of my heart endeavors

Math is never in my favour.


I really want to improve my maths

Because I love this subject

And for this I am trying my best.


I am candid, so I confess

In math exams,

I always create a mess,

All answers I guess.

I get quite less marks.


I believe that if I do better, I will one day

Achieve my goal and I have to seriously

Improve because in our lives maths plays

A very significant role.
