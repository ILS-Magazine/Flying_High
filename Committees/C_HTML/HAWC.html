Under the precious guidance provided by Mrs Sonali Gupta, Principal, ILS and Committee Head, Mrs G Manaise, the team members conducted the following activities, successfully-

  1. Ppts and Videos, related to Corona Awareness and Recent Covid -19 Global Vaccine developments and Trials had been posted regularly throughout the year by the Committee, with an aim to update Corona Awareness among students and to promote Positivity among them.
  2. A video, comprising the members of the Committee, was executed with an aim of being positive for dealing with Covid-19 and the life Post- Corona.
  3. Activities on account of Mental Health Awareness Week, from grades LKG to XII, in October 2020. Slogan writing for grades V and VI, Poster- making for grades VII and VIII, and an Online talk show for students of grades IX to XII on the following topics was organised-
    1. Importance of Mental Health
    2. Seeing a psychiatrist is no longer a Social stigma.
    3. Support for Mental Health
  4. Zenik International Olympiad for Health and Value Systems was conducted from Grades 3 to 10, in the month of October and November 2020. A variety of seminars, zoom meetings, Discussions on Healthy eating habits, Decision making, Failure management and Critical Thinking, and Online test modules were held for giving practice to the students.
