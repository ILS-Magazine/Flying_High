As Jose Eduardo once said “There is no magic wand that can resolve our problems. The solution rests with our work and discipline."

The year 2020 had unexpected plans for us; schooling was limited to the four walls of our room. Maintaining discipline during such an unorthodox routine throughout the day can be a strenuous job. The discipline committee of 2020-21, however, was able to successfully enforce discipline amongst the students of Indian Language School. This could only be possible by utilizing the advantages of online learning, the biggest one of them is the flexibility that it offers. Online learning presents several unique challenges. One of the most significant challenges is to maintain self-discipline. During this problematic period, the heads of the discipline committee (Michelle Singh and Vidit Saxena), along with the other members, came up with various strategies to enforce discipline during online school and ensured the smooth functioning of online classes. These include: forming the code of conduct(a set of guidelines and rules that to be followed by every student attending the online class), monitoring the online classes regularly to ensure serenity and, taking regular sessions with the students to teach them about discipline, to name a few. Despite the overwhelming transition to digital life, the students of Indian Language School have dealt with every challenging situation superbly and have made the most of the academic year 2020. Our teachers too have been working very hard to be up to date with the technology and have made online teaching tremendously interesting and engaging.

To conclude, we would like to thank our principal, Mrs. Sonali Gupta, for her immense support to contribute to the school's success this year. We would also want to take this opportunity to thank our teacher head of the discipline committee, Mrs. Gopal, for supporting us throughout the year in performing all our activities. Lastly, we would like to congratulate and thank the members of the discipline committee for contributing to the success. Good luck to the future discipline committee members and always remember: 

"Discipline is the bridge between goals and accomplishments."

-Michelle Singh & Vidit Saxena. (Heads of Discipline Committee)
