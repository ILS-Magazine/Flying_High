Career counseling is an essential factor for identifying the real potential and guiding students towards the right career path. As members of the career counseling committee, we help students find their interests by holding career counseling sessions every week conducted by professionals and alumni from different career and educational backgrounds from all over the world. This year due to COVID-19 restrictions most of the career counseling sessions had to be held online on Zoom.

On 7th July 2020, a session was held on admission procedures and course options for the US, UK, Canada, and Singapore conducted by our ex-students Natasha Abraham, Mitul Pandey, Jyotsna Hiranandani, and Soumyakanta Sahoo. 

In August 2020 we had a session with Ms. Shubhangi Chaturvedi who has experience of close to 7 years in public relations and corporate communication conducted the career counseling. She very confidently explained the meaning of PR (public relations), it's functioning, advertising, marketing, and human resources. We learned that Public Relations are the practice of deliberately managing the release and spread of information between individuals or an organization and the public. PR is an integrated market, it attempts to blend all aspects of marketing communication such as advertising, sales promotion, direct marketing, and social media, through their respective mix of tactics, methods, channels, media, and activities.

On 23rd October 2020, we had a session with the University of Carleton, Canada. Where the admission head explained the procedure of enrollment into the university, the grades required, and the programs and degrees available. He also explained the living conditions of Ottawa, Canada (where the university is located) and he also gave information about current and future career trends so that students can choose their programs wisely.

On 20th November 2020, a session was conducted with Rohan Muley (Education strategist) and Shikha Gupta (Education Advisor). We learned that in Today’s time we can go in any field of our choice with passion where even a commerce person with his skills can have a career in a science field and vice-versa. New careers such as packaging and designing are also coming up and have huge potential in the future market. For developing an automobile, we not only need an automobile engineer but also IT people and designers. Thus, this proves that now we can choose from a wider range of careers and not from a few common ones. The world is about learning, we should always keep ourselves updated about the various new careers and try to find our passion in various fields and one should not always be focused on trends but also look at their skills and interests.
