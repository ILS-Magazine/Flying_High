Editorial

It is with great pleasure that we bring to you the 2020-21 Flying High Magazine. Although 2020 was an unpredictable year for many of us, it opened us up to so many opportunities. Online school brought about innovations in teaching and learning alike. It seems as though in this past year we have all become tech geniuses with an expanded skill set. We thought it best that the magazine of 2020-21 reflects our experiences from the past year. It is with this spirit that we the students, with the approval of Mrs Sonali Gupta, decided to code this year’s edition of Flying High.


This magazine would not have been possible without our wonderful team:


Gaurav Shah

Nirjhar Datta

This historical edition of Flying High demanded immense hard work. Designing, coding and publishing a website of this scale from scratch is no joke. With over 400 man-hours and over 20,000 lines of code, this magazine is our pride and joy. We are elated to note that this magazine was entirely produced by students. From the articles to the artwork and to this website itself, the students of ILS are making history. We were lucky to have been working under the guidance of many talented teachers including Mrs Gurshan Shergill, Mrs Sudipa Mitra, Mrs Tuhina Bhalekar and Mrs Vandana Chaube. We would also like to give great thanks to the Magazine Committee Head, Mousoomi Shit and the other members of the magazine committee.


Throughout our journey, we were amazed by the talent displayed by our peers. Their artwork was stunning and the articles were profound. This magazine truly gave us an insight into their thoughts and feelings.


We are so excited for you to explore our creation and hope that you enjoy viewing it as much as we did creating it.
